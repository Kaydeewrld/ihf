INGENUITY FOR HUMANITY FOUNDATION

Education is at the heart of our mission. Through initiatives like our Back to School Program, we ensure that underprivileged students gain access to quality education, mentorship, and essential school supplies. Beyond education, we extend our support to widows, offering skill acquisition programs, financial assistance, and emotional support to help them rebuild their lives.
